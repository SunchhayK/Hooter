Hooter

A personal Telegram bot that reads your forwarded scheduling messages, extracts event details using AI, and saves them directly to your Google Calendar.

What Hooter does

  • Parses natural-language schedules forwarded in Telegram chats
  • Extracts event title, start/end time, location, and description
  • Creates events in your Google Calendar automatically
  • Supports timezone-aware scheduling and all-day events
  • Notifies you with a direct link to the created event
Google API usage: Hooter requests access to your Google Calendar solely to create, read, and update events on your behalf. No data is shared with third parties. The app is operated as a private personal tool.

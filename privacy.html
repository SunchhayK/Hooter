Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Telegram Calendar Bot ("we", "our", or "the Bot") handles your personal data when you connect and use the Bot with your Google Calendar account.

1. Information We Access

The Bot requests access to your Google Calendar events (via the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.events scope). This access is used solely to:

  • Create new calendar events based on text messages or forwards you send to the Bot.
  • Query and list upcoming calendar events when you request them via bot commands (e.g., /today, /tomorrow, /schedule).

2. Data Storage and Retention

  • OAuth Tokens: When you authenticate, the authorization token (refresh and access tokens) is saved locally on the secure server hosting the Bot. It is associated with your unique Telegram User ID to authenticate your requests.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your Google Calendar data, credentials, or Telegram messages with any third parties.
  • AI Processing: Messages you send to the Bot are processed using the configured AI provider (Google Gemini or OpenAI) solely to parse the event details (summary, start time, end time, location). This data is not used for model training.

3. Data Control and Deletion

You can revoke the Bot's access to your Google Calendar at any time by:

  • Disconnecting the application directly from your Google Account settings (Security -> Third-party apps with account access).
  • Contacting the bot administrator to delete your stored token file.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the bot owner/administrator.
